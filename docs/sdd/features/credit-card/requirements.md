# Requirements: Credit Card

## Feature Overview
Manage credit cards and their respective invoices.

## User Stories
- As a User, I want to add my credit cards with limits and closing dates.
- As a User, I want to see my available limit and current invoice amount.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [x] Allow creation of credit cards.
- [x] Show credit card limits and current invoice status.
- [x] Ability to pay invoices.\n
